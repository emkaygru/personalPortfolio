About Me

Hi, I'm Emily. I have lived in a few places but I am a Midwest girl at heart. I have a cat and a husband and a burning desire to solve JonBenet Ramsey’s case. In my free time, you’ll find me at Zumba, watching True Crime Documentaries or biking around downtown. That’s just a little about me.

After working in Private, In-Home care for over 10 years, I decided this year’s theme would be “try-something-new-twenty-twenty”. It doesn’t have much of a ring to it, but it sure has given me a new drive to find my next career path. I have always had a love for design and creativity but within the last two years, I have honed in on my skills.

Two years ago, I started teaching myself basic HTML + CSS, Photoshop, and blogging and marketing through social media. From there, I worked for churches doing Social Media Management, Graphic Design (print and web), Web Design, and Communications. Needless to say, these past two years have made me a jack-of-all-trades and I look forward to putting my skills to the test.
